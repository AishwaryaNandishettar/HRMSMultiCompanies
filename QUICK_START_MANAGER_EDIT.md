# Quick Start: Manager Attendance Edit Feature

## 🚀 For Managers - How to Use

### Step 1: Go to Attendance Page
- Login as Manager
- Click on **Attendance Management** from sidebar

### Step 2: Find Absent Employee
- Look for employees with **red "Absent"** badge
- Or use search to find specific employee

### Step 3: Click "Edit" Button
- Find the employee row
- Click the blue **"Edit"** button in the ACTIONS column

### Step 4: Fill in the Form
A modal will open with these fields:

**Status Dropdown:**
- Select "Present" (if worked full day ≥8 hours)
- Select "Half Day" (if worked 4-8 hours)

**Check-In Time:**
- Enter when employee started work
- Example: 09:00

**Check-Out Time:**
- Enter when employee finished work
- Example: 18:00

### Step 5: Save
- Click **"Save Changes"**
- Wait for success message
- Table will refresh automatically

---

## ✅ What Gets Updated

When you mark an absent employee as present:
- ✅ Status changes to "Present" (green badge)
- ✅ Check-in and check-out times recorded
- ✅ Worked hours calculated automatically
- ✅ Attendance type set to "Office"
- ✅ Location marked as "Manager Edited"
- ✅ Will be counted in timesheet and payroll

---

## 🔒 Important Notes

### You Can Only Edit:
- ✅ Your direct reports (employees you manage)
- ✅ Any date (past, present, future)
- ✅ Any attendance status

### You Cannot Edit:
- ❌ Employees from other teams
- ❌ Employees not under your supervision

---

## 💡 Common Use Cases

### Scenario 1: Employee Forgot to Check In
**What to do:**
1. Verify with employee they actually worked
2. Ask them what time they came and left
3. Edit attendance with those times
4. Save

### Scenario 2: App Was Down
**What to do:**
1. Note all employees who couldn't check in
2. Edit each one's attendance
3. Use standard work hours (9 AM - 6 PM) or actual hours

### Scenario 3: Employee Was in Field/Client Site
**What to do:**
1. Confirm they were working (not absent)
2. Edit attendance with approximate work hours
3. System will mark location as "Manager Edited"

---

## 🎯 Quick Tips

### For Accurate Records:
- ✅ Always verify employee actually worked before editing
- ✅ Use realistic work hours
- ✅ Keep your own notes of manual edits
- ✅ Be consistent across your team

### Time Guidelines:
- **Full Day (Present)**: 8-9 hours (09:00 - 18:00)
- **Half Day**: 4-5 hours (09:00 - 13:00 or 13:00 - 18:00)

## ❓ FAQs

**Q: What if I edit the wrong employee?**  
A: Click "Edit" again and revert the changes, or set correct values.

**Q: Can I edit past dates?**  
A: Yes, you can edit any date's attendance.

**Q: Will the employee be notified?**  
A: Not automatically - you should inform them directly.

**Q: Does this affect payroll?**  
A: Yes, edited attendance is counted in timesheet and payroll calculations.

**Q: What if I'm not the employee's manager?**  
A: System will show "Unauthorized" error - only direct managers can edit.

**Q: Can I edit multiple employees at once?**  
A: Currently one at a time - future version may support bulk edit.

---

## 🆘 Troubleshooting

**Error: "Unauthorized"**
- Cause: You're not the reporting manager for this employee
- Fix: Check employee's manager in their profile

**Error: "Employee not found"**
- Cause: Invalid employee ID
- Fix: Refresh page and try again

**Modal doesn't open**
- Cause: Browser cache issue
- Fix: Hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R) or clear cache

**Changes not saving**
- Cause: Backend connection issue
- Fix: Check internet connection, contact IT support
